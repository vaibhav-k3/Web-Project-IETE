IETE Nashik Sub-Centre headed by Prof. Dr.D.M. Chandwadkar bagged second prize in IETE best sub-center category at national level for AY 2016-17.

Welcome to IETE Nashik

IETE Nashik Sub-Centre was opened on 10th September 1999 at the hands of Padmashri P. S. Saran. The centre is active from its inception and regularly organizing expert talks, industrial visits, workshops & technical competition for technocrats. On 22nd June 2000, the website was launched at the hands of Shri Ashok Pradhan, Vice-Chancellor, YCMOU, Nashik for the centre. Many educational institutions have formed IETE Students’ Forum (ISF) due to motivation given by our centre. The increase in the number of memberships shows the interest from society in the activities of us. The activities of the sub-centre were recognized by IETE and awarded best sub-centre award in 1999. For AY 2014-15, IETE Nashik Sub-Centre once again achieved success by bagging second prize in IETE best sub-center category at national level.
The IETE focuses on advancement of the Science and Technology of Electronics, Telecommunication, Computers, Information Technology and related areas. Towards this end the Institution promotes and conducts basic engineering and continuing technical education programmes for human resource development.
