About IETE Nashik

IETE Nashik Sub-Centre was opened on 10th September 1999 at the hands of Padmashri P. S. Saran. The centre is active from its inception and regularly organizing expert talks, industrial visits, workshops & technical competition for technocrats. On 22nd June 2000, the website was launched at the hands of Shri Ashok Pradhan, Vice-Chancellor, YCMOU, Nashik for the centre. Many educational institutions have formed IETE Students’ Forum (ISF) due to motivation given by our centre. The increase in the number of memberships shows the interest from society in the activities of us. The activities of the sub-centre were recognized by IETE and awarded best sub-centre award in 1999. For AY 2014-15, IETE Nashik Sub-Centre once again achieved success by bagging second prize in IETE best sub-center category at national level.The IETE focuses on advancement of the Science and Technology of Electronics, Telecommunication, Computers, Information Technology and related areas. Towards this end the Institution promotes and conducts basic engineering and continuing technical education programmes for human resource development.Nashik center presently has 7 Fellows,31 Members,11 associate members and around 140 student members. The institute conducts Diploma (DIPIETE) and Degree(AMTETE) examinations in two streams i.e. Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering & Computer Science and Engineering in Jun and December every year. This is most suited alternative for diploma and degree courses for the employed and ambitious persons at reasonable costs. These exams are recognized as equivalent by AICTE, various universities,PubliceService Commissions and other reputed institutes in India and Abroad.

About IETE

The Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE) is India's leading recognised professional society devoted to the advancement of Science and Technology of Electronics, Telecommunication & IT. Founded in 1953. The IETE is the National Apex Professional body of Electronics and Telecommunication, Computer Science and IT Professionals. It serves more than 1,25,000 members (including Corporate, Student and ISF members) through various 64 Centres, spread all over India and abroad. The Institution provides leadership in Scientific and Technical areas of direct importance to the national development and economy. Government of India has recognised IETE as a Scientific and Industrial Research Organization (SIRO) and also notified as an educational Institution of national eminence. The objectives of IETE focus on advancing electro-technology. The IETE conducts and sponsors technical meetings, conferences, symposia, and exhibitions all over India, publishes technical journals and provides continuing education as well as career advancement opportunities to its members.
