How To Apply

As Corporate Member - Fill up the membership form (To download membership form please Click Here) and send it to us with required enclosures and fee.

As Student Member - For becoming student member AMIETE / DIPIETE, please send us a draft for Rs. 250/- in favour of secretary General IETE, so that detailed syllabi and membership form for the required category is sent back to you.

20 Reasons to Join IETE

  •   Identify yourself with accomplished and eminent technical fraternity in the country.
  •   Participate in IETE seminars, symposia, conventions, workshops, lectures, conforences, or any event of international, national or local level.
  •   Receive IETE Journal of Research, IETE Technical Review and IETE Journal of Education.
  •   Receive IETE Newsletter free of charge.
  •   Receive recognition in excellence through IETE Awards.
  •   Share ideas and experience with fellow members, authors and reviewers by publishing articles in IETE journals.
  •   Share expertise with fellow members by delivering talks or act as faculty for Centres nearby.
  •   Advertise in IETE Journals.
  •   Networking and interaction with IETE Alumni.
  •   Library facility at all the Centres.
  •   Submit your papers for publication in the appropriate transactions.
  •   Participate in International Conforence Co-Sponsorad by the IETE and major engineering societies.
  •   Register with one or more Societies and participate in Society Sponsored events.
  •   Participate in local events and student events of the IETE.
  •   Entitled to apply to participate in voluntary training courses Sponsored by IETE.
  •   Further more, you can become a IETE reviewer by participating in its reviews.
  •   Read papers in the Transactions in electronics form.
  •   Buy various IETE publications (books, textbooks, handbooks etc.) at member discount rates
  •   Information about papers presented in Technical Group Meetings and Goneral Conferences in the past. If you wish to make a copy of paper, you can do so by visiting the IETE office or related facilities.
  •   Get Membership to a Global professional Organization
